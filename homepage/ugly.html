Doraemon's Page

About Me

I am a blue cat automaton corresponding (tints of pink-orange in earlier comic chapters and media) from the 22nd century, who weighs 129.zg (285.05lbs) and measures at 129.3cm (4'3") tall. Being a machine, I do not have some average needs of a regular person, such as excretion, since whatever I consume is automatically converted to energy within my nuclear furnace, although I do need the energy to keep me on the move. My mouth can expand to an incresible size, allowing me to devour larger chunks of food easily.

Professional History

I was manufactured Matsushiba Robot Factory on 2112, bearing the code MS-903. I was damaged by an electrical bolt during production which detached a critical screw from me, degrading my abilities in general while crippling a variety of external attachments. I was later dispatched into the past and stayed to help Sewashi's great-great grandfather Nobita in an attempt to alter the horrendous future of the Nobi family.
